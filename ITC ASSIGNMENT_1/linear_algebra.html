Course Overview

In this course, we will cover the fundamental concepts of Linear Algebra. This subject is crucial for understanding various mathematical principles and their applications:

  • Vectors: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division
  • Matrices and their properties
  • Determinants and their applications
  • Vector Spaces and their significance
  • Null Space and Column Space
  • Solutions of Systems of Equations

Syllabus

The syllabus for this course includes the following topics:

  • Vector Addition and Subtraction
  • Vector Multiplication and Division
  • Matrices and Determinants
  • Vector Spaces and Null Spaces
  • Column Spaces
  • Solutions to Systems of Equations

We will delve deeply into each of these topics, providing you with the tools needed to understand and apply Linear Algebra in various contexts.
